Platform Direction

RoboLogAI is built to feel less like a news feed and more like an intelligence layer.

The site organizes robotics information around companies, robot profiles, market access, price visibility, deployment signals, country lenses, and source trails. The goal is simple: help readers understand which physical AI systems are real, which companies are gaining momentum, and where the robotics economy is moving.

Industrial, warehouse, medical, delivery, quadruped, and research robotics remain covered as secondary lanes because they explain how autonomy reaches the real world before general-purpose humanoids scale.
