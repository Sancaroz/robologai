About

Events Intelligence

RoboLogAI tracks not only robotics companies and humanoid robots, but also AI and robotics events, fairs, expos, and industry signals. These events help reveal where the robotics ecosystem is moving, which companies are gaining visibility, and what technologies are entering the market.

We follow robotics fairs, AI events, humanoid robot showcases, industrial automation expos, and technology gatherings across Türkiye and global markets, using official event links and participation pages wherever possible.

Events matter because they show real market momentum: product launches, company presence, investor attention, industrial adoption, and the next signals shaping physical AI and robotics intelligence.
