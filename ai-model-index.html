Robotics will not be powered by one model. It will use a stack of reasoning, perception, coding, agents, and deployment tools.

This index is not a consumer chatbot ranking. It asks a robotics-specific question: which AI systems are most useful for building, researching, comparing, and eventually operating real-world machines?

Best starting points

Use ChatGPT or Claude for broad robotics reasoning, Gemini for multimodal inspection, Perplexity for source discovery, and open models for private deployment experiments.
